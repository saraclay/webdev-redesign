[Resource] Sample calendar templates
Below are two sample starter templates if you are stuck on your calendar assignment. Both sample templates have fixed widths. Obviously if you use any of the templates you will need to change the colors to fit your page's color scheme, and fill in content et al to fulfill the requirements of the assignment.

The border template has calendar boxes that all have full 1-pixel colored borders.

The margin template has no borders on the boxes but uses the background color of the outer div to create "borders" around everything.
